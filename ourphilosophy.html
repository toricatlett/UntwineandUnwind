Life can be stressful. Sometimes we just need to unwind from the day we had. This is where our idea for Untwine and Unwind came from. Not only do we love relaxing, but we also like to be creative, so making soaps, bath bombs, and scrubs lets us do both. We use all natural ingredients including essential oils that have calming effects. Once we're done making them, we wrap the products in twine. So when the customer is ready to relax, they can just untwine and unwind.
